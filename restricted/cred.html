Packaging Design

A complete packaging design used for a fictional product.

Initial work was focused on typography and finding the right fonts.
Once the fonts were selected, individual alterations were made in order to fit the overall design and composition.

Next, images were selected in which to create illustrations.
These illustrations represent ingredients found within the product.

The aesthetic implemented in this packaging design is taken from woodcut engravings and contour hatching.
This design also draws inspiration from Victorian-era product packaging and advertisements.

3D Splash Graphic

This graphic was created as a splash image for my consulting website. I chose to include many elements that represent the types of services offered through that consultancy.

First, I began brainstorming ideas and creating vector art to reflect the services provided by Ananda Science Consulting.

Next, I constructed a 3D stage in Blender and began importing vectors from AI. These were all 2D, so I had to create 3D mesh to give them dimension.

In the final steps, I made sure to utilize the same colors implemented in the website. The lighting and camera were placed in the scene and images were exported at medium resolution.
